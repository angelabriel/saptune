saptune 3.1.5 again - Care for some PIE?

April 10, 2025 | By: Sören Schmidt

You may have noticed, that again 3.1.5 appeared in the repositories for SLES for SAP Applications 12 SP5 and 15 SP3-6.
  • 12 SP5: 3.1.5-4.39.2
  • 15 SP3: 3.1.5-150100.8.51.2
  • 15 SP4: 3.1.5-150400.15.22.2
  • 15 SP5: 3.1.5-150400.15.22.2
  • 15 SP6: 3.1.5-150400.15.22.2
The packages only differ in the release part (after the dash), meaning that the package has been rebuild.
To increase security, the saptune binary has been recompiled with -buildmode=pie to be loaded into random locations within virtual memory each time it is executed.
Before I try to explain the background, let people do this for me who are better at it: Position-independent code
Who would have thought that some PIE can be good for you?
